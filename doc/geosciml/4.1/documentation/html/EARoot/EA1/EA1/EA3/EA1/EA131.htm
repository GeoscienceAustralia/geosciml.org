<font color="#4f81bd">CompositionPart</font> represents the composition of a geologic unit in terms of earth material constituents (<font color="#4f81bd">CompoundMaterial</font>).  It decomposes the material making of the unit into parts having distinct roles and proportions.<br/><br/>
