The <font color="#4f81bd">EarthMaterial</font> class holds a description of a naturally occurring substance in the Earth.  <font color="#4f81bd">EarthMaterial</font> represents material composition or substance, and is thus independent of quantity or location. Ideally, <font color="#4f81bd">EarthMaterials</font> are defined strictly based on physical properties, but because of standard geological usage, genetic interpretations enter into the description as well.   <br/><br/>
