The <font color="#4f81bd">ContactDescription</font> provides extended descriptive properties of a geologic contact.  If the contact type is <font color="#4f81bd">ChronostratigraphicBoundary</font>, it can be associated with a geochronologic (i.e., time zone) boundary that may correlate with it.<br/><br/>

«Type» GeochronologicBoundary
Class   		Name:  
  		Name: correlatesWith
The correlatesWith property is an association between ContactDescription and a GeochronologicBoundary describing a geochronologic (i.e., time zone) boundary that may correlate with it. Therefore, a contact correlation with a GeochronologicBoundary SHALL ONLY be allowed when the contactType is a ChronostratigraphicBoundary.
Details:
This associates a physical geologic contact with a geochronologic (ie, time zone) boundary that may correlate with it.<br/>
