A <font color="#4f81bd">BoreholeInterval</font> is similar to a <font color="#4f81bd">MappedFeature</font>  whose shape is 1-D interval and uses the SRS of the containing borehole.  The "<font color="#4f81bd">mappedIntervalBegin</font>" and "<font color="#4f81bd">mappedIntervalEnd</font>" properties are alternative to the 1D geometry to overcome problems with the delivery and ease of queryability of 1D GML shapes.<br/><br/>
