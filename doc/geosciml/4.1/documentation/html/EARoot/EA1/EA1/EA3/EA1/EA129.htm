<font color="#4f81bd">RockMaterial</font> is a specialized <font color="#4f81bd">CompoundMaterial</font> that includes consolidated and unconsolidated materials (such as surficial sediments) as well as mixtures of consolidated and unconsolidated materials.  In GeoSciML Basic, Rock Material is essentially a link to a controlled vocabulary (lithology property) and a color (inherited from <font color="#4f81bd">EarthMaterial</font>).  Specific material properties (and <font color="#4f81bd">CompoundMaterial</font> nesting) are available in GeoSciML Extension.<br/><br/>
