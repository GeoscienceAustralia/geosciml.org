Sampling features are frequently related to each other, as parts of complexes, through sub-sampling, and in other ways. If present, the association class class <i>SamplingFeatureComplex</i> (Figure 9) shall link a SF_SamplingFeature to another SF_SamplingFeature. It shall support one attribute.<br /></p><p>EXAMPLES sampling points are often located along a sampling curve; specimens are usually obtained from a sampling point; pixels are part of a scene; stations are often part of an array. <br /></p><p>This association complements the Intention association which describes relationships between a sampling feature and domain features.   <br /></p>
