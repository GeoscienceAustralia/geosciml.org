prefLabel: CGI Contact Type Categories
definition: This file contains the 201202 version of CGI Contact type vocabulary. Use to populate the contactType property on the GeoSciML Contact feature type. This property categorizes the the type of contact (e.g. intrusive, unconformity, bedding surface, compositional layering, phase boundary) between geologic units for a GSML.GeologicStructure.Contact instance.

    ⇑ -- Alteration facies contact --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/alteration_facies_contact
    Preferred label (en) Alteration facies contact
    Definition () A metasomatic facies contact separating rocks that have undergone alteration of a particular facies from those that have undergone metasomatism of another facies. Alteration is a kind of metasomatism that does not introduce economically important minerals.
    Source (en) this vocabulary
    Relationships:
    Exact match http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/0001
    Broader Metamorphic contact

    ⇑ -- Angular unconformable contact --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/angular_unconformable_contact
    Preferred label (en) Angular unconformable contact
    Definition () An unconformable contact between two geological units in which the older, underlying rocks dip at an angle different from the younger, overlying strata, usually in which younger sediments rest upon the eroded surface of tilted or folded older rocks.
    Source () Neuendorf, K.K.E, Mehl, J.P. & Jackson, J.A. (eds), 2005. Glossary of geology, 5th Edition. American Geological Institute, Alexandria, 779 p.
    Relationships:
    Exact match http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/0002
    Broader Unconformable contact

    Source () Neuendorf, K.K.E, Mehl, J.P. & Jackson, J.A. (eds), 2005. Glossary of geology, 5th Edition. American Geological Institute, Alexandria, 779 p.

    ⇑ -- Conformable contact --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/conformable_contact
    Preferred label (en) Conformable contact
    Definition () A contact separating two geological units in which the layers are formed one above the other in order by regular, uninterrupted deposition under the same general conditions.
    Source () Neuendorf, K.K.E, Mehl, J.P. & Jackson, J.A. (eds), 2005. Glossary of geology, 5th Edition. American Geological Institute, Alexandria, 779 p.
    Relationships:
    Exact match http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/0006
    Broader Depositional contact

    ⇑ -- Contact --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/contact
    Preferred label (en) Contact
    Definition (en) A surface that separates geologic units. Very general concept representing any kind of surface separating two geologic units, including primary boundaries such as depositional contacts, all kinds of unconformities, intrusive contacts, and gradational contacts, as well as faults that separate geologic units.
    Source (en) adapted from Jackson, 1997, page 137; NADM C1 2004
    Relationships:
    Exact match http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/0007
    Narrower Chronostratigraphic-zone contact
    Narrower Faulted contact
    Narrower Geologic province contact
    Narrower Geophysical contact
    Narrower Glacial stationary line
    Narrower Lithogenetic contact

    ⇑ -- Disconformable contact --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/disconformable_contact
    Preferred label (en) Disconformable contact
    Definition () An unconformable contact between two geological units in which the bedding of the older, underlying unit is parallel to the bedding of the younger, overlying unit, but in which the contact between the two units is marked by an irregular or uneven surface of appreciable relief.
    Source () Neuendorf, K.K.E, Mehl, J.P. & Jackson, J.A. (eds), 2005. Glossary of geology, 5th Edition. American Geological Institute, Alexandria, 779 p.

    ⇑ -- Glacial stationary line --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/glacial_stationary_line
    Preferred label (en) Glacial stationary line
    Definition (en) A boundary between a subglacial geomorphic unit and a periglacial geomorphic unit, marking the maximum extent of glacial cover. This can be thought of as the outcrop of the contact between a glacier and its substrate at some time at each point along the boundary. This contact type is included as an interim concept, assuming that in the future, there will be extensions to account better for geomorphic units and line types.
    Relationships:
    Exact match http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/contacttype/0015
    Broader Contact
