    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/metamorphicfacies/amphibolite_metamorphic_facies
    Preferred label (en) Amphibolite metamorphic facies
    Definition (en) Metamorphic facies characterized in rocks of basaltic composition by hornblende-plagioclase (plagioclase more calcic than An17).

    ⇑ -- Pyroxene hornfels metamorphic facies --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/metamorphicfacies/pyroxene_hornfels_metamorphic_facies
    Preferred label (en) Pyroxene hornfels metamorphic facies
    Definition (en) Metamorphic facies characterized in rocks of basaltic composition by clinopyroxene-orthopyroxene-plagioclase (olivine stable with plagioclase).
    Source (en) Smulikowski et al. 2003;Fettes and Desmons 2007
    Relationships:

    ⇑ -- Sanidinite metamorphic facies --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/metamorphicfacies/sanidinite_metamorphic_facies
    Preferred label (en) Sanidinite metamorphic facies
    Definition (en) distinguished from the pyroxene hornfels facies by the occurrence of especially high-temperature varieties and polymorphs of minerals (eg. pigeonite, K-rich labradorite); assemblage for rocks of basaltic composition
    Source (en) Fettes and Desmons 2007
    Relationships:
    Broader http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/metamorphicfacies/metamorphic_facies_not_specified
    Notes:

    ⇑ -- Subgreenschist metamorphic facies --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/metamorphicfacies/subgreenschist_metamorphic_facies
    Preferred label (en) Subgreenschist metamorphic facies
    Definition (en) Metamorphic facies characterized in rocks of basaltic composition by prehnite-pumpellyite, pumpellyite-actinolite, prehnite-actinolite (prehnite and pumpellyite are the diagnostic Ca-Al silicates rather than minerals of the epidote or zeolite groups).
    Source (en) Smulikowski et al. 2003; Fettes and Desmons 2007
    Relationships:
    Broader http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/metamorphicfacies/metamorphic_facies_not_specified
    Notes:

    ⇑ -- Zeolite metamorphic facies --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/metamorphicfacies/zeolite_metamorphic_facies
    Preferred label (en) Zeolite metamorphic facies
    Definition (en) Metamorphic facies characterized in rocks of basaltic composition by zeolite minerals such as laumontite and heulandites (in place of other Ca-Al silicates such as prehnite, pumpellyite and epidote).
    Source (en) Smulikowski et al. 2003; Fettes and Desmons 2007
    Relationships:
    Broader http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/metamorphicfacies/metamorphic_facies_not_specified
