    ⇑ -- brittle deformation --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/deformationstyle/brittle
    Preferred label (en) brittle deformation
    Definition (en) Deformation in fault zone has been accommodated primarily through fracturing and loss of continuity between adjacent rock bodies.

    ⇑ -- ductile deformation style --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/deformationstyle/ductile
    Preferred label (en) ductile deformation style
    Definition (en) Displacement across shear displacement structure largely accommodated by plastic deformation of the rock body without loss of macroscopic continuity.
    Source (en) this vocabulary
    Relationships:

    ⇑ -- mixed brittle and ductile --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifier/cgi/deformationstyle/mixed_brittle_ductile
    Preferred label (en) mixed brittle and ductile
    Definition (en) deformation in fault zone has been accommodated by both fracturing and plastic shape change in rock bodies.
