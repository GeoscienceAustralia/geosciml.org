    ⇑ -- CGI Proportion Term Vocabulary --

    URI http://resource.geosciml.org/classifierscheme/cgi/201012/proportionterm
    Preferred label (en) CGI Proportion Term Vocabulary
    Definition (en) This file contains the CGI Proportion term vocabulary. CGI_ValuePropertyType allows a CGI_TermValue, which may be an uncontrolled string or a scoped name. This vocabulary provides terms that may be used to qualitatively express abundance of composition parts in a geologic unit or earth material description. Quantity that specifies the fraction of the geologic unit composed of the compound material. Might use vocabulary terms like abundant, common, sparse, rare. This vocabulary is used for GeologicUnit.GeologicUnitPart.proportion, GeologicUnit.CompositionPart.proportion, and for CompoundMaterial.ConsituentPart.proportion.
